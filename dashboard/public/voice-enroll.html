Voice Enrollment

Record a clean reference sample of an employee's voice, one guided sentence at a time.

How it works: each recording is cleaned in your browser — high-pass filtered (~85 Hz), silence-trimmed and peak-normalized — before the clips are combined and stored. Nothing raw is uploaded. Note: speaking in a cloned voice requires the F5-TTS backend running on a GPU; until then voices are stored but not yet playable.
